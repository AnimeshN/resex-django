About ResEx

Welcome to the Research Exchange (ResEx) portal, an initiative of the Post Graduate Academic Council (PGAC) of IIT Bombay. Our goal is to increase interdisciplinary research across different academic units within the institute.

The ResEx website is designed to connect students with similar research interests, providing a platform for collaboration and exchange of ideas. By providing information on research equipment and facilities, as well as the faculties associated with each lab, the ResEx website aims to make it easier for students to find and access the resources they need for their research.

We believe that interdisciplinary research is essential for pushing the boundaries of knowledge and addressing some of the world's most pressing challenges. By fostering collaboration across different fields, we hope to create a vibrant research community at IIT Bombay and beyond.

Thank you for visiting the ResEx website and we hope you find it useful in your research journey.
